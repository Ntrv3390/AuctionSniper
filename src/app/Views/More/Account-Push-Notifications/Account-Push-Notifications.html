If you've enabled notifications but they're not being delivered to this device, please check your device settings:

iOS Instructions:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap Auction Sniper
  3. Tap Notifications
  4. Toggle "Allow Notifications" to ON

Android Instructions:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap Apps or Application Manager
  3. Find and tap Auction Sniper
  4. Tap Notifications
  5. Enable notifications
